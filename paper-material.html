Our Paper Cup Range

Choose Your Paper Product

One wall for value. Two for warmth. Three for texture. All for your brand.

Single Wall Cup

Single wall paper cups that are lightweight, practical, and cost-effective. Perfect for cold drinks and short-serve occasions such as juice bars, pop-up events, and quick-service counters.

  • ✓ Great for cold beverages
  • ✓ Lowest cost per unit

Double Wall Cup

Maximum insulation with a premium feel. The air gap between walls maintains temperature longer — the choice for specialty coffee and premium venues.

  • ✓ Maximum insulation
  • ✓ Premium unboxing feel

  • ✓ Comfortable grip
  • ✓ No sleeve needed
